# Tvoiz

**Tvoiz** is a professional, **100% on-device** AI audiobook and speech production studio for macOS. Designed for maximum privacy and performance, Tvoiz runs advanced neural models directly on your Mac's hardware—ensuring your books, voice clones, and personal data never leave your computer.

## 🚀 Private AI Features

- **Local AI Audiobook Studio:** Convert EPUB and PDF libraries into immersive audiobooks using on-device neural synthesis.
- **Local Neural TTS (Kokoro):** Experience studio-quality speech powered by Kokoro and macOS's native Neural Engine (Apple Silicon optimized).
- **Private Voice Cloning:** Create high-fidelity digital voice clones locally. Your reference audio is processed on-device and never uploaded.
- **On-Device Voice Changer:** Transform recordings into any of your private cloned voices using local inference.
- **Smart Local Rewriting:** Refine and naturalize text for speech using local LLMs (via Ollama integration).
- **Zero Cloud, Zero Tracking:** No accounts, no subscriptions, and no internet required for core synthesis. Your privacy is guaranteed by architecture.

## 💻 System Requirements

- **Operating System:** macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later.
- **Architecture:** Universal Binary (Native support for Apple Silicon M1/M2/M3 and Intel).
- **Recommended:** 16GB+ RAM and an M-series chip for the fastest local neural processing.

## 📦 Installation & Security

1. Open the `Tvoiz 1.0.dmg` file.
2. Drag **Tvoiz** into your **Applications** folder.
3. **Security Note:** Since Tvoiz is a private, ad-hoc signed tool:
   - **Right-click (Control-click)** the app in **Applications** and select **Open**.
   - Click **Open** again on the security dialog. This is only required once to authorize the local binary.

## 🎙️ Permissions & Privacy

Tvoiz is built on a "Privacy by Design" principle:
- **Microphone:** Used only for local recording during voice cloning.
- **Speech Recognition:** Performed entirely on-device for local audio transcription.
- **Files:** Tvoiz only accesses the books and audio files you explicitly choose to process.

## 🛠️ Getting Started & Troubleshooting

- **Voice Quality:** For the best experience, ensure you have downloaded the **macOS Premium** voices in *System Settings > Accessibility > Spoken Content*.
- **Local LLM:** Tvoiz integrates with **Ollama** for private text rewriting. Ensure Ollama is installed to enable the "Rewrite" features.
- **No Voices appearing?** Check your Accessibility settings (as above) to ensure at least one high-quality voice is installed.
- **Voice Cloning quality:** For best results, record your reference sample in a quiet environment with a high-quality microphone.
